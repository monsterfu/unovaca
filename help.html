About

App version: $VERSION

nRF Temp is an app designed to receive temperature readings from Nordic Semiconductor's temperature sensors. Advertising temperature value, battery value, and an id directly, these sensors don't require a Bluetooth® connection.

Please note that this app will not receive any measurements if it's in the background.

Usage

To show new sensors, you have to press the add-button in the upper right corner. New sensors will be shown, with their signal strength. Tap the one you want to add, and repeat for all needed sensors. Tapping an added sensor will show you its details and allow you to change its name, see and graph historic data or delete the sensor and its data entirely.
